Strategy

Our main trading targets are stocks, index futures, currencies, and bonds.

We specialize in systematic trading using quantitative models derived from mathematical and statistical analysis.

It predicts the price movements of financial instruments in markets around the world by using complex mathematical models to look for non-random movements in huge amounts of price data.

In addition, many of the processes from analysis to trading are automated.

Risk Management

We use our own risk management model based on the kerry criterion to adjust our position size so that the risk is always within a reasonable range.

Our Risk Management team oversees all aspects of both financial and non-financial risks including Market and Liquidity Risk, Counterparty Risk, Model Risk, Operational Risk, and Technology Risk.

We also have multiple locations all over the world to be able to respond to any situation such as disaster or machine trouble.
